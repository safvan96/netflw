Applications & Expertise

Flow and level measurement, engineered for your industry

Electromagnetic flow meters

From DN3 to DN3000, our electromagnetic flow meters measure water, wastewater, chemicals, slurry and food-grade liquids with no moving parts and no pressure loss. Six liners and six electrode alloys cover everything from drinking water to aggressive acids.

Level instruments & switches

Magnetic bypass level indicators, float transmitters and level switches for tanks, boilers, silos and hazardous areas, reliable point and continuous level measurement, with or without power.

Calibration & global delivery

Every instrument ships with TÜRKAK-accredited ISO/IEC 17025 calibration, recognized in 100+ countries. We export to Iraq, the Gulf, Central Asia and beyond with direct engineering support and short lead times.

About Net Flow

A subsidiary of VFA Elektronik

Net Flow is an international export brand of VFA Elektronik, a Turkish manufacturer founded in 2012 in Konya, Turkiye. VFA Elektronik specialises in the design, manufacture and calibration of electromagnetic flow meters, level sensors, and process instrumentation for water, wastewater, chemical, food and industrial applications.

Our production facility houses a TURKAK-accredited calibration laboratory (AB-0354-K, ISO/IEC 17025:2017), enabling every instrument to be factory-calibrated to traceable national standards. All products carry Yerli Mali (local-production) certification, ISO 9001 quality management and CE marking.
